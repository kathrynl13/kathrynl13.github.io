Chapter 1: Wildlife

There is much wildlife to be cautious of when hiking. Chapter 2: Communication

When hiking cellular is not always available. It is important to always notify friends and family that you are going hiking, when you are leaving, what hike you are doing, and when you expect to return. This way if you do not return, they can inquire with authorities. It is a good investment to purchase a satellite phone which allows you to contact others no matter where you are.
