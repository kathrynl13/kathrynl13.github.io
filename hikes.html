Hike 1: Devils Thumb

Devils thumb is a beautiful hike located at Lake Louise. At the top you receive are breathtaking view of two takes. The hike takes around two hours to drive there from central Calgary. It is nearly 13 km with almost 900m. We stopped at the top of Big Beehive for double views. We complete this hike July 1, 2022.

Hike 2: Ptarmigan Cirque

Ptarmigan Cirque is a great hike for beginners. This hiking is in Kananaskis just over an hour and half drive from central Calgary. There is no super hard terrain on this hike. This short hike is only 3.5km with 250m of elevation gain. We completed this hike on August 4, 2022.

Hike 3: Heart Creek Bunker

Heart Creek Bunker Trail is located at the base of Heart Mountain. Only an hour drive from central Calgary this is a great choice if you like the dark. Make sure to bring flashlights because the bunker is complete dark and goes in more than you would expect. The hike itself is rated easy only being 2.5km and low elevation. We completed this hike on September 6, 2022.
