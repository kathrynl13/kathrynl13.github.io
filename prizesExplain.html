User Signs Up

Users receive 1 JStoken the moment they connect their wallet address to Stollayto and deposit a minimum of 100 USDT into any liquidity pool. The address is linked to a phone number to ensure people can only receive 1 JStoken upon signing up.

Random Drop Model

After the liquidity pool reaches a certain threshold (>$10K), Stollayto will randomly drop 1 JStokens to all users to incentivize users to remain active on the platform and reward them.
