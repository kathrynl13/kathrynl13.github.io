Secret Santa Swap

Secret Santa Swap, is designed to allow groups of people to participate in Secret Santa gift exchanges effectively through anonymous communication and wish list capabilities online. The platform supports the creation of password protected groups, where the creator of the group can initiate assigning Secret Santas once all members have joined the group. Note: this was a group acedemic project.

Technologies: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap, MongoDB, Socket IO, Node.js, Mongoose, Express, Axios
