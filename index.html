Welcome Hikers!

We are hiking adventures! We are a group mostly located in Calgary, Canmore, Strathmore, Cochrane, Airdrie, and Chestermere. Members are of all skill levels. Together we help promote a healthy and active lifestyle by hitting the mountains every month. Please fill out the information on the contact section if you would like to become part of our group. We hiking the first Saturday of every month depending on weather conditions.
